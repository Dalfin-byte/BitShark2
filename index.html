    Security Tips

    • Always use a unique password for every account.
    • Change your passwords regularly, especially if you suspect a breach.
    • Consider using a password manager to keep track of your credentials securely.
    • Never share your passwords or write them down in insecure locations.
    • Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible for added security.
